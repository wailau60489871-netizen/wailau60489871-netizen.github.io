Pizza Express

Brand Activation & Multi-Channel Design
Long-term collaboration delivering visual solutions across multiple brand touchpoints — from in-store campaigns and digital charity promotions to experiential installations and food content production.

Campaign & Charity Activation

Delivered campaign visuals supporting brand partnerships and promotional initiatives, including Pizza Express collaborations with Movember.
  • Designed campaign posters and promotional formats
  • Developed package-based and on-pack visual applications
  • Created in-store display materials and campaign headlines
  • Translated restaurant launches into immersive campaign spaces

Social Media & Content Production

  • Produced campaign-led social content across food, beverage and branding campaigns
  • Created and captured product photography
  • Directed lighting for promotional visuals
  • Managed lifestyle storytelling for post-production
  • Adapted assets for social-first formats
  • Maintained consistent brand tone across campaign touchpoints

Environmental & Experiential Design

Delivered large-scale environmental graphics and experiential brand installations across retail and event environments.
  • Designed store event executions blending messages
  • Developed outdoor event backdrops
  • Created wayfinding and pop-up store visuals
  • Produced install design and graphic implementation layouts
This work extended the brand beyond print into immersive physical experiences.

Menu Design & In-Restaurant Touchpoints

Designed and managed menu format applications, in-layout brand execution, tray liners and branded identity across dine-in environments.

  • Created seasonal and everyday menu systems
  • Developed and organised menu photography
  • Designed in-store table materials and supporting POS graphics
  • Maintained visual consistency across printed and physical touchpoints

Across multiple brand phases, consistency remained central, ensuring brand guidelines translated into cohesive dining experiences.
