KMB

Recruitment Campaign &
Outdoor Brand Application

Developed a recruitment-focused campaign for KMB, translating a clear hiring message into high-visibility outdoor and transit environments. The project focused on communicating employment opportunities through bold visual messaging, strong typographic hierarchy and transport-led imagery that could be immediately recognised in busy urban contexts.

Campaign Concept &
Visual Direction

The campaign was designed to function across multiple physical formats, including large-scale building banners, bus body advertising and street-level recruitment touchpoints. Each execution required careful adaptation of layout structure, ensuring the key message remained highly legible at distance while maintaining brand clarity and visual impact.

Transit Advertising Application

Large-Format Outdoor Application

Strong emphasis was placed on information hierarchy, balancing promotional messaging with practical recruitment details such as contact information and communication channels. The design approach prioritised message clarity, immediate recognition and strong public visibility within high-traffic environments.


Beyond the core visual concept, the project also involved adapting campaign artwork across different scales and physical placements. From large architectural banners to moving transit media, the campaign maintained a consistent visual system while responding to the spatial and environmental constraints of each application.

Street-Level Recruitment Touchpoints

The final outcome demonstrates how a unified design approach can extend across outdoor, transit and street-level formats, transforming everyday urban environments into effective communication platforms while reinforcing the visibility and accessibility of the recruitment message.
