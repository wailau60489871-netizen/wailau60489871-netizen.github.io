Video Projects

This collection presents a range of moving-image projects including brand storytelling, advertising campaigns, social media content and product launch films. From concept development and storyboard planning to directing, filming and post-production, each project explores how motion design can extend visual communication into narrative experience.

Samaroli Karuizawa

This film was created to introduce a rare Japanese whisky release bottled by Samaroli in Scotland. The product originates from a historic Japanese distillery that has already closed, making the remaining stock extremely rare.

The project combines product design, packaging storytelling and cinematic imagery to reflect the cultural fusion between Japanese heritage and Scottish whisky craftsmanship.

PizzaExpress 20th Anniversary TVC

This short film celebrates the 20th anniversary of PizzaExpress in Hong Kong. The concept focuses on the iconic Romana pizza and uses exaggerated storytelling to highlight the unique personality of the product.

From concept development and storyboard planning to directing and post-production, the entire project was executed collaboratively with the production team.

Social Media Reels

This short-form social media reel captures the journey of freshly baked doughballs from the kitchen to the customer’s table.

The concept combines practical filming with playful post-production effects, transforming a prop doughball into a full-sized edible doughball to create a surprising visual moment.

The Whisky Library Pop-up Store

This teaser film was created to promote the opening of The Whisky Library pop-up store in central Hong Kong, showcasing the design features of the temporary retail installation.

Due to strict location restrictions the shoot had to take place after 11pm and be completed within four hours using minimal production equipment.

Mixian Sense Product Launch

A commercial film produced for a Hong Kong rice noodle restaurant chain, focusing on warm brand storytelling and expressive food cinematography.

YouTube Music Project

An independent music video produced during the pandemic with musician friends. The film reflects how everyday moments became unexpectedly difficult during that time.

Kee Wah Mooncake Production

This film documents the mooncake production process for a long-established Hong Kong brand. The narrative follows the journey from pastry preparation to the finished products reaching stores.
