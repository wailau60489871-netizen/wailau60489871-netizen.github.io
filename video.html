Video & Motion Projects

Visual Storytelling Through Motion

This collection presents moving-image projects across brand storytelling, campaign communication, food production, social-first content and independent filmmaking. The work spans concept development, storyboard planning, directing, cinematography, editing, colour treatment and visual finishing.

Samaroli Karuizawa

This film was created to support the launch of a rare Japanese whisky release bottled in Scotland by Samaroli. The product originated from a historic Japanese distillery that had already closed, making the remaining aged stock highly limited and culturally significant.

My role began with the development of the label and packaging design, where the visual direction was built around the fusion of Japanese and Scottish influences. The film extended this same design language into motion, translating the product’s rarity, heritage and collectable value into a cinematic visual narrative.

Through close-up product cinematography, controlled lighting and a more atmospheric pacing, the film was designed to express the sense of preciousness and cultural depth behind the release, reinforcing both its premium positioning and its storytelling value.

PizzaExpress 20th Anniversary Film

This short film was created to celebrate the 20th anniversary of PizzaExpress in Hong Kong. The concept centres on the popularity of the brand’s signature Romana pizza, using a playful anthropomorphic approach to exaggerate and visualise the product’s distinctive characteristics.

The narrative follows a group of characters whose personalities reflect different qualities of the Romana pizza, turning familiar product features into humorous visual metaphors. Through this stylised storytelling approach, the film transforms a simple food promotion into a light-hearted and memorable brand narrative.

From concept development and storyboard planning to directing the shoot, editing and visual effects, the production was developed collaboratively with the team to create a cohesive campaign film celebrating the brand’s milestone.

PizzaExpress Social Media Reels

This short-form social media reel was created to highlight the journey of PizzaExpress doughballs, from the kitchen to the customer’s table. The film captures the freshness and warmth of the product while maintaining the playful visual tone associated with the brand.

The concept centres on a visual transformation where a prop doughball used during filming gradually transitions into a real, edible doughball through post-production. This approach blends practical filming with visual effects to create a seamless and engaging visual moment.

Designed for social media viewing, the reel emphasises quick pacing, strong visual clarity and food-focused imagery, turning a simple product moment into dynamic short-form content.

The Whisky Library — Pop-Up Store Launch Film

This teaser film was produced to promote the opening of The Whisky Library’s pop-up store in central Hong Kong. The objective was to introduce the temporary retail space through a cinematic preview that highlighted the craftsmanship and unique design features of the installation.

Due to location restrictions, filming could only take place after 11pm and had to be completed within a four-hour window without the use of heavy production equipment. The challenge required a compact production setup while still achieving a polished visual result.

The film focuses on the transformation elements within the pop-up store design. A wooden case gradually unfolds and reveals itself as an elegant display counter, demonstrating the modular structure and refined aesthetic of the retail concept.

Rice Noodle Restaurant Commercial

This commercial was produced for a Hong Kong noodle restaurant chain, focusing on elevating the brand’s image through a warm and approachable narrative.

From concept development and storyboard creation to directing the shoot and post-production, the project was developed collaboratively with the production team to deliver a cohesive advertising piece.

The casting featured a rising young actress whose natural screen presence and friendly personality helped convey the welcoming character of the brand. Combined with carefully shot food preparation scenes, the film highlights both the craftsmanship of the dishes and the inviting atmosphere of the restaurant.

Independent Music Video (Pandemic Project)

This music video was produced during the pandemic as part of an independent YouTube channel created with a group of musician friends. The project explored how visual storytelling could reflect the emotional atmosphere of the period while working under extremely limited production conditions.

Inspired by the song’s lyrics, the film portrays how simple everyday activities became unexpectedly difficult during the pandemic, turning quiet moments into the central narrative of the piece.

With no production budget available, the entire shoot was executed independently using a minimal setup. Operating the camera and lighting alone required constant on-set adjustments to achieve the desired visual tone while maintaining a cinematic feel.

Mooncake Production Film

This film documents the mooncake production process for a long-established Hong Kong food retail brand, capturing the craftsmanship behind the product while presenting the company’s commitment to quality and professional food production.

From concept development and storyboard planning to filming and post-production, the entire project was independently produced, shaping both the narrative and the visual tone of the final film.

The key creative challenge was translating the cold factory environment into a film that still conveys warmth and human craftsmanship. The visual narrative follows the process from pastry preparation to the final products arriving in retail stores, highlighting the precision and care behind every stage of production.
