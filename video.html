Samaroli Karuizawa

This film was created to support the launch of a rare Japanese whisky release bottled in Scotland by Samaroli. The product originated from a historic Japanese distillery that had already closed, making the remaining aged stock extremely limited and culturally significant.

The project combined packaging design and cinematic storytelling. The visual narrative reflects the fusion between Japanese heritage and Scottish whisky craftsmanship, translating the rarity and cultural value of the product into a refined visual experience.

PizzaExpress 20th TVC

This short film celebrates the 20th anniversary of PizzaExpress in Hong Kong. The concept focuses on the brand's iconic Romana pizza, using exaggerated storytelling to highlight the product’s distinctive character.

The project involved concept development, storyboard creation, directing and post-production. Through character-driven scenes and stylised humour, the film transforms product features into engaging visual metaphors.

Social Media Reels

This short-form reel was created for social media platforms, focusing on the journey of freshly baked doughballs from kitchen preparation to the customer’s table.

The visual concept integrates practical filming with post-production effects, transforming a prop doughball into a real edible doughball to create a playful visual moment.

The Whisky Library Pop-up Store

This teaser film was produced to promote the launch of a whisky pop-up store in central Hong Kong. The video highlights the design details and spatial atmosphere of the temporary retail installation.

Filming could only take place after 11pm with a four-hour window and minimal equipment, creating a production challenge that required a compact and efficient shooting setup.

Mixian Sense New Product Launch TVC

This commercial was produced for a Hong Kong rice noodle restaurant chain. The concept highlights the warmth and approachability of the brand through expressive food cinematography and character performance.

From storyboard development to directing, filming and post-production, the project was executed collaboratively with the production team to create a visually engaging advertisement.

YouTube Channel

This independent music video was produced during the pandemic with musician friends. The project explored how everyday moments became unexpectedly difficult during this period.

With no production budget available, the entire shoot was executed independently using a minimal camera and lighting setup.

Kee Wah TVC

This film documents the mooncake production process for a long-established Hong Kong food brand. The narrative follows the journey from pastry preparation to the final products arriving in retail stores.

The challenge was to convey warmth and craftsmanship within the cold environment of a modern food factory, highlighting the care and professionalism behind the production process.
