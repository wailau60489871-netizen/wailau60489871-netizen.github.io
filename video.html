Video & Motion Projects

Visual Storytelling Through Motion

This collection presents moving-image projects across brand storytelling, food content, campaign communication and social-first production. The works combine concept development, filming, editing and visual finishing to transform real-world processes and spaces into engaging visual narratives.

Samaroli karuizawa

A short-form product film developed to extend the visual atmosphere of the packaging into motion. The piece focuses on mood, texture and brand tone, combining close-up imagery with a controlled cinematic pace to support premium product storytelling.

The project explores how packaging visuals can be translated into moving image content while maintaining a consistent luxury aesthetic across digital touchpoints.

Pizzaexpress 20th TVC

A social-first reel developed to bring campaign energy into motion through quick edits, lifestyle framing and a food-led visual rhythm. The short-form format is designed for continuous playback, allowing the film to function as an attention-grabbing digital asset within the page itself.

Social media reels

A collection of short-form vertical and social-led video content developed for digital platforms. These pieces focus on immediacy, visual rhythm and compact storytelling, adapting brand, food and lifestyle subjects into highly accessible motion content.

The work demonstrates how editing pace, framing and concise narrative structure can be used to optimise content for mobile viewing and platform-based distribution.

The Whisky Library Pop up store

A spatial and brand-focused film documenting the visual atmosphere of a retail pop-up environment. The piece captures material details, interior mood and environmental composition, transforming a physical space into a cinematic brand experience.

Mixian sense New Product Launch TVC

A launch-focused promotional video created to introduce a new product through clean visual composition and product-led storytelling. The film balances human presence and product detail to build a warm and contemporary viewing experience.

The final piece is structured to support launch communication across both digital display and campaign distribution.

Youtube Channel

An ongoing video channel project covering a variety of content types, including lifestyle documentation, urban visuals and observational storytelling. The work reflects a flexible approach to moving image production, balancing spontaneity with careful framing and edit rhythm.

Kee Wah TVC

A short production film documenting the making of traditional mooncakes, focusing on craft detail, food texture and process-based storytelling. The project was independently developed from concept and storyboard planning through filming, lighting and post-production.
