Samaroli

Packaging System
Premium Packaging System & Brand Execution
Role: Lead Designer — Packaging & Brand Rollout

The Challenge

Refined and systemised the label structure to improve hierarchy, scalability and production consistency, while preserving Samaroli’s heritage aesthetic.
  • Maintaining brand legacy while appealing to a modern audience.

My Role

  • Led the refinement and development of Samaroli’s label system.
  • Developed a scalable packaging framework across multiple releases.
  • Directed typography, layout and visual hierarchy decisions.
  • Managed print production specifications and supplier communication.
  • Oversaw proofing, colour calibration and material testing.
  • Defined and maintained packaging guidelines for future editions.

Label System Development

Label System Development

A structured and scalable label framework was developed to ensure consistency across multiple Samaroli releases while allowing illustration and narrative variation.

Core Label Structure

  • Defined clear hierarchy between brand, release and technical information
  • Established a repeatable grid structure
  • Balanced illustration placement within a controlled frame

Grid & Typographic Refinement

  • Adjusted typographic weight and spacing
  • Tested illustration scale within fixed margins
  • Standardised information blocks for future releases

Application Across Editions

The label system was consistently applied across subsequent release, maintaining structural integrity while allowing each edition to retain its own visual identity.

Application Across Editions

  • Cohesive brand recognition
  • Scalable production workflow
  • Long-term design continuity

Production &
Material Strategy

Production & Material Strategy

A considered production approach reinforced the brand’s premium positioning through material and finish decisions.

Foil, UV & Surface Detailing

  • Specified gold foil stamping for brand emphasis
  • Applied spot UV to enhance illustration contrast
  • Controlled emboss depth to balance tactility and legibility
  • Tested reflective finishes under retail lighting conditions

Paper & Texture Selection

  • Selected textured stock to reflect artisanal character
  • Evaluated paper weight and grain direction
  • Reviewed ink absorption and colour consistency

Proofing & Print Accuracy

  • Reviewed digital and physical proofs
  • Calibrated colour consistency across batches
  • Ensured alignment precision on multi-layer finishes

Digital & Campaign Rollout

Brand Extension & Digital Rollout

The packaging system was extended across digital and campaign touchpoints to maintain a unified brand presence.

Social Media Assets

  • Developed launch visuals for limited-edition releases
  • Art-directed product photography for social platforms
  • Maintained a consistent brand tone across campaign touchpoints

Website Application

  • Adapted packaging hierarchy for product-led web layouts
  • Ensured consistency between physical packaging and online presentation
  • Maintained typographic and visual alignment across product pages

Campaign Film & Social Rollout

Led the end-to-end production of a short-form launch film to support the limited-edition release.

  • Developed the initial creative concept and visual narrative
  • Directed product cinematography and lighting
  • Managed filming, editing and post-production
  • Optimised content for social-first distribution

Catalogue Design

  • Structured product spreads using the established typographic hierarchy
  • Maintained consistent visual rhythm across editorial layouts
  • Ensured clarity of technical information within premium layout formats

Retail & Physical Application

  • Applied brand consistency across wooden presentation boxes
  • Managed packaging alignment between bottle, box and printed collateral
  • Reinforced premium positioning through cohesive physical detailing

Before / After Evolution

The evolved label system strengthened typographic hierarchy and improved structural clarity while preserving brand heritage.

Key Improvements

  • Clearer typographic hierarchy
  • Improved spacing and alignment
  • More structured information layout
  • Enhanced production detailing
  • Greater scalability across future editions
