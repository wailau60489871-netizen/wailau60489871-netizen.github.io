Samaroli Whisky Label Design

Samaroli is one of the most respected independent whisky bottlers in the world. This project explores the visual identity and label system created for a range of limited edition whisky releases. The design focuses on typography hierarchy, structured label systems and premium printing production to create a distinctive and collectible packaging experience.

Label System

Each release follows a structured label framework that balances heritage aesthetics with modern clarity. Typography hierarchy, edition numbering and production information are carefully arranged to ensure both readability and premium presence.
