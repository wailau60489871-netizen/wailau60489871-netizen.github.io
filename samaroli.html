Samaroli

Samaroli is one of the most respected independent whisky bottlers in the world. This project explores the development of a refined packaging system that balances heritage aesthetics with modern typographic hierarchy. The work focuses on label structure, print detailing and visual storytelling to elevate the perception of each limited edition release.

The design approach began with exploring the visual balance between traditional whisky label aesthetics and contemporary layout clarity. Typography hierarchy and structured spacing were carefully developed to ensure both readability and strong shelf presence.

Each edition required subtle variation while maintaining a consistent visual system across the range. This structure allows future releases to evolve while preserving the recognisable Samaroli identity.
