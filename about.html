About

I am a multidisciplinary designer working across branding, packaging, visual storytelling and motion.

My work explores how design systems can translate brand ideas across different touchpoints — from labels and printed materials to campaigns, environments and film.

Projects range from packaging and brand development for Samaroli to campaign visuals and digital content for PizzaExpress, often combining design thinking with creative direction and production.

I am interested in building visual languages that remain consistent across physical and digital platforms while retaining strong narrative clarity.

Working Approach

My practice combines concept development, visual design and production execution.

I enjoy working across disciplines — from graphic design and typography to photography, video and environmental graphics — allowing projects to develop as cohesive visual experiences.
