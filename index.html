Samaroli

A premium packaging system created for limited-edition whisky releases, focused on hierarchy, label structure and refined production detailing.

The project balances heritage aesthetics with a more structured visual system, allowing multiple editions to feel consistent while maintaining their own identity.

The work extends beyond the label into boxes, campaign visuals and supporting digital applications.
