PizzaExpress is one of the most recognisable restaurant brands in the UK, known for its distinctive dining experience and strong visual identity.

This project explores the development of campaign visuals and branded content designed to enhance the in-restaurant experience while extending the brand narrative across digital and social platforms.

The work combines food photography, visual storytelling and graphic design to create engaging campaign materials that celebrate the atmosphere and personality of the brand.
